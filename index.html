ABOUT


My name is Michael. I'm an aspiring full stack developer originally from the north east of England, but now settled in the San Franciscio Bay Area. I am currently working through the Free Code Camp cirriculum and then intend to apply to a coding bootcamp to further sharpen my development skills. My original background is in HVAC and commercial sales and I hope to translate my skills from these industries into the tech industry. I love interacting with customers and developing the most robust and creative solutions to meet their needs.

Outside of my development time I am an avid mountain biker. Currently my favorite place to ride is Santa Cruz but I still miss riding in the beautiful forests of Soctland near where I grew up. I was born in the city of Newcastle Upon Tyne and have supported the Newcastle United Football Club since my youth. I am now begining to follow Bay Area sports as well and very much enjoy watching American football and basketball.
